How to get a visa

No “100% guarantees”. Just a clear process — and we tell you upfront what is realistic and what is not.

1) Define the purpose of travel Tourism, business, visiting family/friends — the purpose determines the visa type and required documents.
2) Check requirements Every country has its own rules, timelines, and details. We explain them clearly before you start.
3) Prepare documents Forms, insurance, bookings, translations — consistent and accurate, without contradictions.
4) Submission and waiting Appointment booking, submission guidance, and instructions until the final result.
5) Visa decision You receive the decision and practical recommendations before your trip.
