FAQ

Do you guarantee a 100% visa approval?

No. The final decision is made by the consulate. We prepare your documents carefully to reduce risks, but we do not promise the impossible.

How long does the process take?

On average from 1 to 6 weeks depending on the country, season and document readiness.

Is it possible to apply without hotel/flight bookings?

For most countries you need confirmed bookings. We arrange refundable bookings and insurance tailored to your package.
