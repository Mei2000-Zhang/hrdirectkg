Visa FAQ — Common Questions

Clear answers, no false promises.

Do you guarantee a 100% visa approval?

No. The final decision is always made by the embassy or consulate. We prepare a correct application and reduce risks, but we do not promise the impossible.

How long does the visa process take?

It depends on the country and appointment availability. On average, from a few days up to 4–6 weeks. Exact timelines are discussed during consultation.

Do you help with forms and appointments?

Yes. We complete visa application forms, check documents, and assist with appointment booking when required.

What are the most common reasons for refusal?

Errors in the application form, weak financial proof, inconsistencies in travel purpose, or poor visa history.

Can I reapply after a refusal?

In most cases, yes. The key is to understand the refusal reason and correct the application strategy.
