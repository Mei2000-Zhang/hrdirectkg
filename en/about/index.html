Who we are

HR Direct KG is a consulting company in Bishkek working with visas and overseas employment. Our job is to make your process organized: correct forms, consistent documents, and realistic expectations.

What we do

  • Visa consultation and strategy (based on purpose and documents)
  • Application forms completion and data verification
  • Insurance tailored to travel dates and requirements
  • Support with bookings and package structure (when needed)
  • Employment consultations and process coordination

Destinations

• Schengen — tourism, business, private visits
• Japan — document package, guidance, consistency
• UAE (Dubai) — e-visa submission support
• China — tourist/business, invitations guidance

Why clients choose us

  • We don’t promise “100% approval” — we promise accuracy
  • We catch typical mistakes before submission
  • We explain what is realistic in your case
  • We keep communication simple and clear
