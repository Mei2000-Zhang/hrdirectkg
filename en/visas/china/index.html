China Visa

Tourism and business — calmly, by the rules and without chaos

China has strict requirements for documents. We prepare a logical package, advise on invitations and guide you step by step until submission.

What we do:
• Selecting the visa type according to the travel purpose
• Application forms and document verification
• Assistance with invitation (if required)
• Clear timeline and step-by-step plan

China visa types

The two most common options are tourism and business. We will help determine which one fits your travel purpose.

Tourist visa

For travel, sightseeing, holidays and personal visits without business activity.

  • passport + photo
  • application form (we help fill it out)
  • travel / accommodation confirmation (bookings)
  • financial proof: bank statement or proof of funds (depending on the case)
*The exact list depends on the case and submission requirements.

Business visa

For negotiations, exhibitions, meetings with partners and business trips.

  • passport + photo
  • application form
  • invitation from the Chinese side (if required)
  • employment / company documents (depending on the case)
*If you do not have an invitation, we will explain available options.
Important: “Invitation” and “insurance” are not visa types. They are additional elements that may be required depending on your situation.
