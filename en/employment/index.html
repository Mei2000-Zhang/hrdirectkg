What we provide

  • Vacancy matching based on your profile
  • Explanation of requirements and conditions
  • Document guidance and checklist
  • Support through the process

We focus on clarity and compliance — not “fast promises”.

What you should avoid

  • Buying “fake invitations” or fake documents
  • Agreeing to unclear contracts
  • Paying money without receipts / terms
  • Leaving without understanding your role and schedule

Shortcuts usually become expensive later.

Transparency note

  • Employer conditions are discussed before start
  • Timelines depend on documents and quotas
  • Final decision belongs to employer/authorities

We tell you “yes/no” honestly, not emotionally.

How we work

Simple structure — and you always know what’s next.

1) Inquiry You message us with basics: country, sector, experience, age, language skills (if any).
2) Matching We propose available options and explain conditions, timelines, and documents.
3) Preparation We provide a clear checklist and help avoid typical mistakes.
4) Coordination We stay in touch and guide you through each stage until the result.

Basic requirements (usually)

Exact requirements depend on country and vacancy. Below is the typical minimum.

Passport (valid for the required period)
Personal documents (ID, certificates if needed)
Readiness to work shifts / physical tasks
Clean and honest information in documents
Basic language (optional, depends on employer)
Discipline and reliability (always required)

If you are not sure what you qualify for — message us, and we will suggest realistic options.
